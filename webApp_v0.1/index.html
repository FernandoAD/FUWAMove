The problem

We know that it can be difficult to get around Furtwangen and that many times you would like some transportation alternatives. Some points of the town may be difficult to reach or it may be very tiring to move to certain points on foot.

What is FUWA Move?:


The bicycle is a very fun means of transport and it can be quite useful if it is used correctly, that is why we are working on offering possibilities with bicycles for students.
Not everyone has the possibility of having their own car, but we have the willingness that students have to enter a shared route program and in this way, their car has a better use.
It is planned according to what the students demand, to try to organize small buses with defined routes to some of the most important points of the town.
